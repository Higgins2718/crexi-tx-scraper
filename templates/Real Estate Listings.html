TEXAS INDUSTRIAL MARKET UPDATE – NEW CREXI LISTINGS HIT THE MARKET

THE TEXAS INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE SCENE IS HEATING UP AS A DIVERSE GROUP OF NEW LISTINGS ARRIVES ON CREXI. FROM MAJOR HUBS LIKE HOUSTON AND SAN ANTONIO TO EMERGING MARKETS IN CORPUS CHRISTI, MIDLAND, AND BEYOND, THERE’S PLENTY TO CATCH AN INVESTOR’S EYE.

NOTABLY, A STANDOUT OPPORTUNITY IS COMING IN AT 6455 CUNNINGHAM RD IN HOUSTON. MARKETED UNDER NAN AND COMPANY PROPERTIES, THIS EXPANSIVE 22,608‑SQUARE‑FOOT MIXED-USE ASSET—SPANNING INDUSTRIAL, SELF‑STORAGE AND SPECIAL PURPOSE USES—IS LISTED AT AN EYE‑POPPING ASKING PRICE OF $1.1 BILLION. WHILE THE PRICE TAG IS UNEXPECTED, ITS POTENTIAL FOR VARIED COMMERCIAL APPLICATIONS MAY MAKE IT A UNIQUE, HIGH‑PROFILE LANDMARK FOR THE MARKET.

OVER IN CORPUS CHRISTI, TWO INDUSTRIAL OPPORTUNITIES MAKE WAVES. ON LIPAN STREET, CRAVEY REAL ESTATE SERVICES INC HAS INTRODUCED A RENOVATED 48,970‑SQUARE‑FOOT PROPERTY THAT TAPS INTO THE PORT CITY’S GROWING INDUSTRIAL DEMAND. IN A COMPLETELY DIFFERENT FORMAT, GARRON DEAN & ASSOCIATES IS OFFERING A 4.7‑ACRE SITE ON SOUTHSIDE CORPUS CHRISTI WITH A MASSIVE 422,532‑SQUARE‑FOOT AREA—DEMONSTRATING THE BROAD SPECTRUM OF SCALE NOW AVAILABLE IN THE REGION.

SMALLER-SCALE PROPERTIES PROVIDE EQUALLY INTRIGUING PROSPECTS. KELLER WILLIAMS REALTY AUSTIN’S 6,000‑SQUARE‑FOOT FACILITY IN FLORENCE AND THE PRICHARD GROUP’S 5,000‑SQUARE‑FOOT WAREHOUSE/SHOP WITH AN OFFICE SUITE IN MONAHANS PRESENT NIMBLE OPPORTUNITIES ACROSS THE STATE, EACH PRICED ATTRACTIVELY FOR OWNER/USER INVESTORS.

SAN ANTONIO CONTINUES TO BE A HOTBED OF ACTIVITY WITH TWO KEY LISTINGS. TEXAS TU CASA’S 4,000‑SQUARE‑FOOT WHOLESALE PRODUCE MARKET ON SE LOOP 410 HIGHLIGHTS TENANT‑ACTIVE INDUSTRIAL RETAIL USES, WHILE CBRE – SAN ANTONIO BRINGS FORWARD A HYBRID OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL SPACE AT 204 N WW WHITE BOASTING AN IMPRESSIVE 55,628‑SQUARE‑FOOT FOOTPRINT FOR INNOVATIVE, MIXED‑USE OPERATIONS.

ELSEWHERE, REGIONAL PLAYERS ARE CAPTURING ATTENTION IN MARKETS LIKE EL PASO, RED OAK, WICHITA FALLS, TYLER, HUMBLE, AND MIDLAND. FOR INSTANCE, RJL REAL ESTATE CONSULTANTS’ DUAL‑TYPE RETAIL/INDUSTRIAL PROPERTY AT 517 S OREGON ST IN EL PASO AND KELLER WILLIAMS REALTY FORT WORTH’S MIXED‑USE FACILITY IN RED OAK—WITH 3,000 SQUARE FEET OFFERED AT NEARLY $500K—OFFER SMALLER, FOCUSED INVESTMENTS. MEANWHILE, DOMAIN REAL ESTATE SERVICES INC’S 30,625‑SQUARE‑FOOT INDUSTRIAL SPACE IN WICHITA FALLS AND THE ADAPTIVE REUSE OF THE FORMER LONE STAR EVENT CENTER IN TYLER (12,000 SQUARE FEET) ILLUSTRATE CREATIVE SOLUTIONS TO MEET EVOLVING DEMAND. IN HUMBLE, PARTNERS’ NEW CONSTRUCTION OF OVER 10,350 SQUARE FEET SIGNALS FRESH DEVELOPMENT IN THE HOUSTON AREA, AND STRAHAN COMMERCIAL PROPERTIES’ 10,300‑SQUARE‑FOOT WAREHOUSE IN MIDLAND ROUNDS OUT OUR UPDATE WITH ANOTHER SOLID REGIONAL PLAY.

THESE NEWEST CREXI LISTINGS UNDERSCORE THE DYNAMIC AND DIVERSE NATURE OF TEXAS’S INDUSTRIAL MARKET. WHETHER YOU’RE LOOKING FOR LARGE-SCALE, MIXED‑USE DEVELOPMENTS OR MORE FOCUSED, BOUTIQUE INVESTMENTS, THERE’S SOMETHING ON THE TABLE FOR EVERY TYPE OF MARKET PARTICIPANT. AS INDUSTRIAL DEMAND CONTINUES TO SPUR INNOVATION AND REDEVELOPMENT, THESE PROPERTIES COULD WELL BE THE NEXT BIG OPPORTUNITIES IN THE LONE STAR STATE. STAY TUNED FOR MORE UPDATES AS THESE LISTINGS—AND OTHERS—MAKE THEIR MARK ON THE TEXAS INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE LANDSCAPE.
